Rule 1
General Provisions

1.1
Scope of the Rules
The Rules of Golf apply to playing golf in stroke play and match play.

1.1a
Authority of the Committee
The Committee has authority to interpret the Rules and resolve disputes.

1.2
Standards of Player Conduct
Players must act with integrity, show consideration for others and play with care.

Rule 2
The Course

2.1
Definitions
The course is the entire area of play within the boundaries set by the Committee.
